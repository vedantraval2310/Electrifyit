Creating an Agency

If you're a new user, you'll need to create an agency to get started. Follow these steps:

Navigate to the "Login" section in the Landing page.

Fill out the agency details, including name and description

Click "Create" to create your agency

Managing Agency Settings

As an agency owner, you have full control over your agency settings. Here's how to manage them:

Navigate to the "Settings" section in the dashboard

Click on "Agency Settings"

Update your agency information, including name, description, and logo

Click "Save Changes" to apply your updates
